From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This list comprises the world's largest companies by consolidated revenue, according to the annually ranked Fortune Global 500 published by Fortune magazine, as well as other sources.[2] Out of 51 largest companies 23 are American, 17 Asian and 11 European.[3]

This is limited to the largest 50 companies, all of which have annual revenues exceeding US$130 billion. This list is incomplete, as not all companies disclose their information to the media or general public.[4] Information in the list relates to the most recent fiscal year (mostly FY 2023 or 2024).
